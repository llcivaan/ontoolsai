What percentage of painting quotes convert?
28–35% without follow-up, rising to 48–55% with a 3-message sequence. The key insight: colour indecision is the #1 cause of silence, not price. A follow-up that addresses this converts far better than a generic check-in.
Why do painting customers delay accepting a quote?
Colour indecision accounts for over 40% of delays. Price is second. Timing and disruption are third, especially for interior jobs. Address the real blocker in your follow-up — not just "have you decided yet?"
What is the difference between interior and exterior painting jobs?
Interior is disruption-sensitive — address timing flexibility in follow-ups. Exterior is weather-sensitive — genuine weather windows create real urgency that converts well when used honestly.
When should a painter ask for a Google review?
Interior: on the final day, as the last coat dries and furniture goes back. Exterior: the morning after, once they've seen it in daylight. Both have a strong visual reveal moment — ask within hours of it, not days later.
