What percentage of landscaping quotes convert?
One-off jobs convert at 35–45% without follow-up, rising to 60–65% with a prompt follow-up. Larger design quotes convert at 25–35%. Recurring maintenance contracts are the most valuable — once won, they compound monthly.
When is the busiest season for landscaping businesses?
Spring (March to May) is peak demand. Autumn (September to October) is the second peak for maintenance contracts. Winter is slow — smart businesses use it to lock in spring contracts.
How valuable is a recurring landscaping maintenance contract?
At $120/month, a single recurring customer generates $1,440/year with minimal re-selling. Converting one-off customers to contracts is the highest-leverage sales activity for any landscaping business.
How many Google reviews does a landscaping business need?
40+ reviews at 4.5 stars wins local search. 83% of homeowners check reviews before hiring. Reviews mentioning specific services — lawn care, garden design, hedge trimming — rank well for those local searches.
