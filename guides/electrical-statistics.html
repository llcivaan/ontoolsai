What percentage of electrical quotes convert?
Emergency jobs convert immediately. For planned work, conversion without follow-up averages 30–38%, rising to 52–60% with a structured sequence. Trust-led follow-ups mentioning qualifications and compliance consistently outperform generic ones by a significant margin.
How important are Google reviews for electricians?
Critical. 86% of homeowners check reviews before hiring. Because electrical work is safety-critical, trust signals carry more weight than in most trades. Reviews mentioning safety, compliance certificates, or tidy workmanship are especially influential.
What are the most common electrical jobs?
Power point and light installation, safety switch upgrades, switchboard upgrades, EV charger installation, and full rewiring are most common. EV charger installs are the fastest growing category in most markets.
What certifications does an electrician need to show customers?
A Certificate of Compliance (or jurisdiction equivalent) is required for notifiable electrical work. Mentioning your licence number and compliance certificate process in follow-up messages significantly increases trust and conversion rates.
