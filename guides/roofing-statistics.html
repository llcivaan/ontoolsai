What percentage of roofing quotes convert to jobs?
Industry benchmarks show 20–30% of roofing quotes convert without follow-up. With a structured 3-message sequence over 3–4 weeks, conversion rates rise to 45–55%. Large re-roof quotes respond best to follow-ups that address decision timeline and materials questions rather than just asking for an answer.
How many Google reviews does a roofing company need?
40+ reviews at 4.5 stars consistently wins the majority of local search clicks. Reviews mentioning specific job types — re-roof, storm damage, flat roof — rank particularly well for those searches.
What is the average roofing job value?
Residential repairs range from $300 to $3,000. Full re-roofs average $8,000 to $25,000. Storm damage and insurance jobs frequently exceed $15,000. These high values make every unconverted quote extremely costly — a single recovered quote can represent a month of smaller trade revenue.
When is roofing season?
Spring (March–June) and early autumn (September–October) are peak seasons in most markets. Summer brings storm-driven emergency demand spikes. Winter is slow except for urgent leak repairs — smart businesses use it to build a spring pipeline.
How long does a typical roofing job take?
Most repairs take one day. Full residential re-roofs take two to four days. Multi-day jobs need proactive daily communication — a brief update significantly reduces inbound enquiries and complaint risk.
