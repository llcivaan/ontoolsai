Cookie Policy

Last updated: March 30, 2026

This Cookie Policy explains how OnToolsAI uses cookies and similar local storage technologies on ontoolsai.com.

1. Do We Use Cookies?

OnToolsAI does not use tracking cookies or advertising cookies. We do not use Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, or any third-party tracking scripts on our main application.

2. What We Use Instead — localStorage

Our application uses your browser's localStorage to store the following data locally on your device:

  • ontoolsai_usage — your message count for the current month
  • ontoolsai_plan — your current plan (free, pro, or business)
  • ontoolsai_email — your email address if you signed in via paid plan
  • ontoolsai_bonus_claimed — whether you have claimed your 5 bonus messages
  • ontoolsai_bonus_email — email provided to claim bonus messages
  • ontoolsai_name, ontoolsai_business — your name and business name for auto-filling message fields

This data is stored entirely on your device and is not transmitted to our servers (except your email address when you voluntarily provide it).

3. Third-Party Cookies

The following third-party services may set their own cookies when you interact with them:

  • Lemon Squeezy — sets cookies when you visit the checkout page to process your payment
  • Google Fonts — may set cookies when loading fonts used on our website

4. How to Clear Your Data

You can clear all locally stored data at any time by clearing your browser's localStorage for ontoolsai.com. In most browsers: Settings → Privacy → Clear browsing data → Cached/local data.
