The communication gap Jobber leaves open

Jobber has automated reminders — they fire when an invoice is due and send basic follow-up nudges. But there is a meaningful difference between an automated generic template and a message that sounds human, specific to the situation, and written for your trade.

When a customer goes quiet after a quote, Jobber's template says "just following up." OnToolsAI writes a message that gives the customer a reason to respond — references the specific job, offers flexibility, and signals confidence rather than desperation. That difference is the gap between a 15% and a 35% quote conversion rate.

When an invoice is overdue, Jobber sends a reminder. OnToolsAI writes three escalating messages calibrated to preserve the relationship at every stage — because how you word the second and third reminder matters as much as sending them at all. Jobber knows your jobs. OnToolsAI knows what to say about them.
