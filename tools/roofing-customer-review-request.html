Review request templates

After a repair or one-day job

Template 1 — Same day completion
Hi [Name], [Your name] from [Business]. Really glad we got the [repair / job] sorted for you today. If you're happy with the work, a Google review would mean a lot to us — it helps other homeowners find us when they need help. Here's the link: [your Google review link]. Thanks — [Your name]
✓ Send within a few hours of completion · Keep it short

After a full re-roof or multi-day job

Template 2 — Larger job, final day
Hi [Name], [Your name] from [Business]. Great to get the [re-roof / job] finished today — the team did a brilliant job and I hope you're as happy with the result as we are. If you have 60 seconds, a Google review genuinely helps our business — here's the link: [your Google review link]. Thanks for trusting us with it. — [Your name]
✓ Send on the final day once site is clean and scaffold is down

After an insurance or storm damage job

Template 3 — Insurance or storm job
Hi [Name], [Your name] here. Glad we could get the storm damage sorted quickly for you — I know it was a stressful time. If you'd be willing to leave a Google review mentioning the work we did, it really helps homeowners in similar situations find us. Here's the link: [your Google review link]. Much appreciated. — [Your name]
✓ Storm damage customers are often the most motivated to leave reviews — the stress was real
💡 Roofing SEO tip: Encourage customers to mention the specific job type in their review — "re-roof", "flat roof repair", "storm damage", "leaking roof". Reviews with specific keywords help your business appear in those exact local searches. A prompt like "feel free to mention what we did" in your message often gets more descriptive reviews.

When should a roofing company ask for a Google review?
On the final day of the job, once the site is clean and the customer has seen the finished roof. For larger jobs, wait until the scaffold is down and the garden is tidy — the customer's impression of 'done' matters.
How should a roofing company ask for a Google review?
By text, on completion day, with a direct Google review link. Keep it personal and reference the specific job. Roofing customers who are happy are often very happy — a simple, direct ask converts well.
How many Google reviews does a roofing company need?
40+ reviews at 4.5 stars consistently wins local search. Reviews mentioning specific job types (re-roof, flat roof, storm damage) rank particularly well for those searches and are worth encouraging.
What makes a roofing Google review valuable for SEO?
Reviews mentioning specific job types act as keyword signals for local search. Encourage customers to describe what was done — not just give a star rating. Each descriptive review is significantly more valuable for local SEO than a rating-only review.
