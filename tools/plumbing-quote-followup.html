Frequently Asked Questions

How do I follow up on a quote without sounding pushy as a Plumbing business owner?

Keep it simple and frame your follow-up as a helpful check-in rather than a sales call. Ask if they have questions rather than asking if they're ready to buy. OnToolsAI generates professional plumbing quote follow-up messages that hit the right tone every time.

How long should I wait before following up on a plumbing quote?

Two to three business days is the sweet spot — long enough to give the customer time to consider, short enough that your quote is still fresh. If you sent the quote on a Friday, follow up on Tuesday morning.

What should I say when following up on a plumbing quote?

Reference the quote date, offer to answer questions, and make it easy to respond. Avoid pressuring them with urgency or discounts on the first follow-up. A short, friendly message almost always outperforms a long sales-style email.

Should I follow up by text or phone call for a plumbing quote?

Text or email works best for the first follow-up — it's low pressure and easy for customers to respond in their own time. Save the phone call for a second follow-up if you haven't heard back within another few days.
