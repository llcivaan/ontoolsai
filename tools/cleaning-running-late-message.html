Cleaner Running Late Message Templates

The short answer: A running-late message sent before the client starts waiting turns frustration into understanding. For cleaning clients — especially those who've taken time off work or arranged to be home — punctuality matters a lot. Send the moment you know you're running late, not when you're already late.

By OnToolsAI · Updated March 24, 2026 · 8 min read

Short delay — 15 to 30 minutes

For short delays, one sentence is enough. The client just needs to know you're coming and roughly when.

Template 1 — Short delay, simple
Hi [Name], [Your name] from [Business]. Quick heads-up — I'm running about [15-20] minutes behind today. Should be with you by [revised time]. Really sorry about that! — [Your name]
Template 2 — Short delay, with reason
Hi [Name], sorry — the job before yours ran over a bit. I'm on my way and should be there around [time] instead of [original time]. Appreciate your patience! — [Your name]

Longer delay — 45 minutes or more

For significant delays, give the client the option to reschedule. Don't assume they can wait.

Template 3 — Longer delay, offer to reschedule
Hi [Name], [Your name] here. I'm so sorry — I'm running considerably behind today and realistically won't be with you until around [time]. I completely understand if that doesn't work for you — I can reschedule for [day/time] if that's easier. Just let me know what you'd prefer. — [Your name]

When you need to cancel on the day

Day-of cancellations damage trust, especially with recurring clients. Be honest, apologise sincerely, and offer a specific rescheduled slot rather than asking them to get back to you.

Template 4 — Day-of cancellation
Hi [Name], I'm really sorry — [brief reason] means I won't be able to come today. I know this is inconvenient and I'm genuinely sorry. Can I come [tomorrow / first thing on X day] instead? I'll make sure to fit you in as a priority. — [Your name], [Business]

What should a cleaner say when running late?
Keep it short — revised arrival time, brief apology, and optionally a one-line reason. Don't over-explain. 'Running about 20 minutes behind — should be with you by 10:30. Really sorry for the delay' is perfect. Longer explanations read as defensive rather than considerate.
When should a cleaning business send a running-late message?
The moment you know you're going to be late — not when you're already late. Clients who are notified in advance are almost universally forgiving of delays. Clients who have to chase you when you haven't shown up are the ones who leave bad reviews.
How late is too late for a cleaning appointment?
For cleaning clients, delays beyond 30-45 minutes typically warrant offering to reschedule rather than just updating the arrival time. Some clients have firm commitments around their cleaning appointment. Give them the option rather than assuming they can wait.
