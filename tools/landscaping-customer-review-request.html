Review request text templates

Landscaping has one of the strongest visual satisfaction triggers of any trade — customers can see the transformation immediately. That emotional high is your review window. Use it.

After a lawn cut or garden tidy

Template 1 — Same day, after a tidy or cut
Hi [Name], [Your name] from [Business] — really glad the [lawn / garden] came up so well today. If you've got 60 seconds, a Google review would mean a lot to us — it helps other homeowners find us. Here's the link: [your Google review link]. Thanks — [Your name]
✓ Send within 2 hours of finishing · Keep it short and personal

After a garden redesign or installation

Template 2 — After a larger job, give it a few days
Hi [Name], [Your name] here. Hope the [new garden / planting / lawn] is all settling in well — it was a great project to work on. If you're happy with how it's looking, we'd really appreciate a Google review. Here's the link: [your Google review link]. Only takes a minute. Cheers — [Your name]
✓ Send 3-5 days after a design or installation — let it settle first

For a recurring maintenance customer

Template 3 — Loyal recurring customer
Hi [Name], [Your name] from [Business]. We've really enjoyed looking after your [garden / lawn] this season — if you ever had a spare minute to leave us a Google review, it genuinely helps us a lot. No pressure at all — here's the link if you'd like to: [your Google review link]. Thanks as always — [Your name]
✓ Use after a few months of regular visits · Loyal customers leave the best reviews
💡 Take a before-and-after photo. Send it with your review request — "here's how it looked when we arrived vs now." Customers who see the visual transformation in the message are significantly more likely to follow through with a review, because you've reminded them exactly what they're praising.

When is the best time to ask a landscaping customer for a Google review?
Within a few hours of completing the job — while the garden looks its best. Landscaping has a strong visual satisfaction trigger: the before-and-after is immediately visible. Asking the same day converts at significantly higher rates than asking days later.
How should a landscaper ask for a Google review?
By text, personally, on the day of the job. Reference what you did, make a direct ask, include the Google review link. One sentence is enough. A direct link removes the most common drop-off point — customers who have to search for you rarely complete the review.
How many Google reviews does a landscaping business need?
40+ reviews at 4.5 stars consistently wins the majority of local search clicks. Review recency matters too — a business with 15 recent reviews often outperforms one with 50 older ones in local search rankings.
What if a landscaping customer leaves a bad review?
Respond professionally within 24 hours. Acknowledge the issue, offer to make it right, move the conversation offline. A calm, professional response to a negative review often converts more browsers to callers than a perfect rating.
Is it better to ask for a review by text or email for landscaping?
Text converts at significantly higher rates. SMS open rates are around 98% versus 21% for email. Landscaping customers are often at home when the job is done — they read the text immediately and follow the link while the positive feeling is fresh.
