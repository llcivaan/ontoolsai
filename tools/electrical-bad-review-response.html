Frequently Asked Questions

How should I respond to a bad Google review for my electrical business?

Stay calm, acknowledge the issue, and offer to resolve it offline. Never argue in public — other customers are reading your response. OnToolsAI generates professional electrical bad-review responses that protect your reputation while showing empathy.

What should I NOT say when responding to a bad review?

Never blame the customer, make excuses, or get defensive. Avoid sharing private customer details. A defensive or emotional response can do more damage than the original negative review.

Does responding to bad Google reviews actually help my electrical business?

Yes — research consistently shows that businesses that respond to negative reviews earn more trust from potential customers. A well-handled complaint is often more persuasive than a perfect score.

How quickly should I respond to a negative Google review?

Within 24 hours if possible. A fast, professional response signals to future customers that you're attentive and take your work seriously. The longer a negative review sits unanswered, the worse it looks.
