Frequently Asked Questions

How do I follow up with a customer who missed their roofing appointment?

Keep the message neutral and non-accusatory. Assume something came up on their end and make it easy to rebook. OnToolsAI helps roofing businesses send professional no-show follow-ups that recover bookings without conflict.

How do I protect my roofing business from customer no-shows?

Send a confirmation text 24 hours before the appointment and a reminder 1 hour before. This alone can reduce no-shows by over 50%. Consider implementing a cancellation policy for repeat no-shows.

Should I charge a no-show fee as a roofing business?

It's reasonable to have a policy, but be careful with first-time no-shows — a gentle message goes a long way. Consider charging a fee only after the second missed appointment.

What's the best way to reschedule a roofing customer who didn't show up?

Make it easy — provide a phone number or reply option and suggest a couple of available times. Reduce any friction in the rebooking process and you'll recover most no-show bookings within 24 hours.
