Frequently Asked Questions

How do I ask for a Google review without being annoying as a Electrical business owner?

Timing is everything. Ask immediately after completing a successful job while the customer is still happy. Keep the message short and include a direct link. OnToolsAI helps electrical businesses send the right review request at the right moment.

When is the best time to ask a customer for a Google review?

Right after you finish the job — ideally within the hour. The customer's satisfaction is at its peak and they're most likely to take action. Waiting even a day can significantly reduce your response rate.

How do I get the Google review link to send to my electrical customers?

Search for your business in Google Maps, click on your listing, then click 'Get more reviews' in the Overview tab. Copy that link and save it — it's the direct link customers can use to leave a review without searching for you.

What's a good Google review request message for a electrical business?

A great message thanks the customer, mentions the specific job, and includes a direct link. Keep it under three sentences. Personal touches like using their first name make a big difference in response rate.
