Frequently Asked Questions

How do I handle a customer complaint professionally as a Electrical business owner?

Listen first, apologise for the experience (not necessarily the fault), and offer a concrete resolution. Avoid making excuses. OnToolsAI generates professional complaint-response messages for electrical businesses that de-escalate and rebuild trust.

What should I say to an angry electrical customer?

Acknowledge their frustration, thank them for raising the issue, and focus on solutions. Don't get defensive or emotional. Most customers want to feel heard more than they want compensation.

Should I offer a refund when a electrical customer complains?

Not automatically — offer to return and fix the issue first. Many customers just want the problem resolved correctly. Jumping straight to refunds can actually feel dismissive of the quality of your work.

How do I turn a complaint into a loyal customer in my electrical business?

Respond quickly, take ownership, fix the issue, and follow up afterwards to confirm they're satisfied. Customers who have a complaint resolved well are often more loyal than those who never had a problem at all.
