The Situation

An invoice is past due. You need to follow up without damaging the relationship or coming across as aggressive.

Copy-Paste Template

Why This Message Works

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I remind a customer to pay an invoice without damaging the relationship?

Assume the best — treat it as an oversight rather than intentional non-payment. Be clear about what's owed and how to pay, but keep the tone friendly. OnToolsAI helps cleaning businesses send professional invoice reminders that get paid faster.

How many days past due should I wait before sending a payment reminder?

Send the first reminder on the due date or one day after. Most late payments are genuinely forgotten, not deliberate. A gentle same-day reminder gets better results than waiting a week.

What should I include in an invoice reminder message?

Include the invoice number, amount owed, original due date, and how to pay. Keep the message short — customers are more likely to act on a clear, simple message than a long, detailed one.

How do I get cleaning customers to pay faster?

Offer multiple payment options (bank transfer, card, cash), set clear payment terms upfront, and follow up promptly when payments are late. Automated reminders via text message tend to get faster responses than emails alone.
