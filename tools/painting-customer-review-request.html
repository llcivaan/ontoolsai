Review request templates

Interior painting — completion day

Template 1 — Interior job, final day
Hi [Name], [Your name] from [Business]. Really glad the [lounge / bedroom / whole house] came up so well — it was a great job to work on. If you have 60 seconds, a Google review would mean a lot to us. Here's the link: [your Google review link]. Thanks — [Your name]
✓ Send while they're still in the room admiring it · Reference the specific space

Exterior painting — day after completion

Template 2 — Exterior job, morning after
Hi [Name], [Your name] here. Hope the exterior is looking great in the morning light — it was a brilliant transformation. If you're happy with the result and have a spare minute, a Google review would really help us. Here's the link: [your Google review link]. Cheers — [Your name]
✓ Exterior: send the morning after so they see it in daylight before you ask

After a positive check-in response

Template 3 — Two-step: check-in first, then ask
Really glad to hear you're happy with it! If you'd ever have 60 seconds to leave us a Google review, it makes a big difference for our business — here's the link: [your Google review link]. Thanks again — [Your name]
✓ Use after they respond positively to a check-in message · Highest conversion rate
💡 Painting SEO tip: Ask customers to mention what was painted and where in their review — "interior of 3-bedroom home in [suburb]" or "exterior repaint". These specific keywords make the review far more valuable for local search than a generic 5-star rating.

When is the best time to ask a painting customer for a Google review?
On the final day of the job, once the last coat is dry. The customer walks into the freshly painted room and sees the transformation. Asking while they're in that reveal moment converts at much higher rates than asking days later.
How should a painting contractor ask for a Google review?
By text, on completion day, with a direct Google review link. Reference what was painted and keep the ask to one sentence. For exterior jobs, send the morning after so they've seen it in daylight first.
What makes a good painting review for local SEO?
Reviews mentioning the specific job type and location — "interior painting in [suburb]", "exterior repaint" — act as keyword signals for local search. Encouraging descriptive reviews is significantly more valuable than ratings-only reviews.
