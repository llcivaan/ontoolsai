The honest truth about Housecall Pro's pricing

Housecall Pro's advertised entry price of $79 per month (or $59 per month annual) looks reasonable until you look at what is not included. Multiple independent reviews in 2026 consistently flag the same issue: the features most home service businesses actually need are not on the Basic plan.

⚠️ The Housecall Pro add-on problem

QuickBooks sync, GPS tracking, and two-way texting all require upgrading beyond the Basic plan. Common add-ons — including the flat rate price book ($149/mo), Sales Proposals ($40/mo), and Vehicle GPS ($20 per vehicle per month) — can push total monthly costs well above the advertised price. Most users on the Basic plan end up paying significantly more than $79 per month once they activate the features they actually need.

This context matters because when trade business owners search for Housecall Pro alternatives, they are often not looking to replace the whole platform — they are looking for specific features at a lower cost.
